# AI Curriculum — Course Catalog

Proposed course titles for the corporate AI education program. Each line is a candidate module; we'll sequence, scope, and assign audiences later.

## Foundations

- AI Fundamentals
- How LLMs Work (Tokens, Embeddings, Transformers — no-math version)
- The AI Landscape: Models, Providers, and Trade-offs
- AI Ethics, Safety, and Responsible Use
- Data Privacy & Compliance for AI (GDPR, data residency, PII handling)

## Working with LLMs

- Prompt Engineering
- Context Engineering
- Structured Outputs and JSON Mode
- Tool Use & Function Calling
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Evaluation & Testing of LLM Applications
- Cost, Latency, and Token Economics

## Agents & Automation

- Introduction to AI Agents
- Background Agents
- Multi-Agent Systems & Orchestration
- MCP (Model Context Protocol)
- Skills (Reusable Agent Capabilities)
- Workflow Automation with AI

## AI in the IDE & Developer Productivity

- Claude Code for Developers
- Cursor, Copilot, and the Modern AI IDE
- AI-Assisted Code Review
- Testing and Debugging with AI
- Refactoring Legacy Code with AI

## AI for Non-Technical Roles

- AI for Managers & Executives
- AI for Product Managers
- AI for Sales & Marketing
- AI for Customer Support
- AI for HR & Recruiting
- AI for Finance & Operations
- AI for Legal Teams

## Building AI Products

- Designing AI-First Products
- UX Patterns for AI Applications
- Choosing the Right Model for the Job
- Fine-tuning vs. Prompting vs. RAG
- Deploying AI to Production
- Observability and Monitoring for AI Systems
- Security for AI Applications (prompt injection, jailbreaks, data exfiltration)

## Media & Creative AI

- Image Generation (Midjourney, DALL·E, Stable Diffusion)
- Video Generation and Editing with AI
- Voice, Speech, and Audio AI
- AI for Content Creation & Marketing

## Organizational Adoption

- Building an AI Strategy for Your Company
- Change Management for AI Adoption
- Identifying High-ROI AI Use Cases
- Training Your Team: Internal AI Enablement
- Measuring AI Impact & KPIs

## Hands-on Workshops

- Build Your First Chatbot
- Build a RAG System Over Company Documents
- Build a Custom Agent with MCP
- Automate a Real Workflow in One Day
